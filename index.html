About Bnook

Bnook is the only Ebook reader that makes reading in a foreign language simple.

Whether you've been studying for weeks or for years, whether you have several teachers or whether you're your own teacher, reading in a foreign language can be tough. Bnook takes away the frustration and inconvenience of reading in a foreign language and makes it just that: reading.
